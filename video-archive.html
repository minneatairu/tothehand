Beginning on 16th October 2023, OUR CHANNEL will broadcast five LIVE stations dedicated to pivotal moments spanning STYLE, MUSIC, FILM, CULTURE and NEWS, as selected by Martine Rose and friends.

Our Channel Text

Our Channel revives a lost culture using modern tools, a time capsule of how life felt when SSENSE was born. This was the era of landlines, fanzines, peer-to-peer downloads, pirate radio and TV shows like The Word, Network 21, and Dance Energy.

This narrative continues as we dive deeper into the evolution of culture through the lens of creativity, collaboration, and identity. Join us as we reimagine pivotal moments and celebrate their lasting impact on modern culture.

Beginning in 2023, this project will also host curated playlists, digital archives, and exclusive interviews to bridge the past with the present.
